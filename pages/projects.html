HeavenNest, meticulously built with Ruby on Rails, SCSS, JavaScript, PostgreSQL, and Active Storage, offers a gateway to extraordinary accommodations worldwide. From vibrant city apartments to peaceful escapes, our platform ensures a seamless booking experience.
The user-friendly interface, powered by Bootstrap and SCSS, and interactive elements enhanced by Stimulus, provide smooth navigation. Behind the scenes, PostgreSQL and Active Record handle data efficiently, while Devise ensures secure authentication and user management. Whether you're browsing or booking, HeavenNest is your trusted companion for finding a place where every destination feels like home!

PopCorn, meticulously crafted with Ruby on Rails, Bootstrap, and PostgreSQL, is your go-to platform for managing and discovering movies. Effortlessly browse and organize your favorite films with a seamless user experience. Our responsive design, powered by Bootstrap and Simple Form, ensures a fluid and intuitive interface.
Active Record structures relational data efficiently, while Active Storage handles image uploads with ease. Devise guarantees secure user authentication and management, allowing peace of mind while exploring. With real-time movie data from the TMDB API, PopCorn keeps you up-to-date on the latest films.
