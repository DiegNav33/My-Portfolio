Short summary of my life !

Born and raised in Bordeaux, France, I was exposed to a myriad of cultures and design expressions. I developed an interest in photography and video design from a young age, but it was the diversity of landscapes, cultures, and architecture that we can find around our planet that stole my heart and took me around the world as soon as I finished school. After a career in the automobile field I decided to learn the web development and today I am proud to be qualified as a web developer.

I'm a detail-oriented problem solver who enjoys tackling complex challenges, and I'm always looking for opportunities to improve my skills and expand my knowledge. Whether it's learning a new programming language or exploring the latest web development tools and frameworks. I'm up for the challenge.

When I'm not pushing codes, you'll find me working out in the park, cooking, surfing or discovering new places around our beautiful earth.
